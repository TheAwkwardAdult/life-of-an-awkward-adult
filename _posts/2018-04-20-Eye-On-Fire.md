## An ode to the stye.
_I got a stye,_

_In my eye._

_Alas. I can't deny._

_Feels like a fly,_

_Sitting on my eye._

_All I can do is lie,_

_Or sigh._

_Oh my._

_I want to die._

_I see through my window a guy,_

_Getting high._
